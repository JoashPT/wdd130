History

The company is a result of the ambitions of Willhelm von Reines as he visited the Philippines and became enamored with its natural beauty.

As a nature enthusiast, he went on to explore the different parts of the Philippines and during his travels, he was inspired to enable others to experience the same rush of the rivers of the Philippines.
