Overview

Purpose

Attract customers to purchase service from my company for White Water Rafting activities. Inform customers about White Water Rafting activities and how my company helps people experience it.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Experienced, Beginner, or just an Enthusiast? We have White Water Rafting trips for all levels!

What questions will be answered by accessing the website?
- What is White Water Rafting?
- Who are allowed to join?
- How much does a trip cost?
- What are the promos/trips available?
- What are the credentials of this company?
- What are the previous customers feedback to their experience in our service?

What do they want?
A fun and memorable water trip with your friends or family.
